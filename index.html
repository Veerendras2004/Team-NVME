Dive into a World of Events

Unveiling a Kaleidoscope of Talent at Our College!

Explore the vibrant tapestry of festivities at our college! From cultural extravaganzas to intellectual showdowns, we have multiple fests that showcase the diverse talents of our students. To discover the exciting event details, simply click on the link below and immerse yourself in the spirit of celebration:

Our Events

FIESTA

UVCE Fiesta, the cultural fest at the University Visvesvaraya College of Engineering, is a spectacular celebration of creativity and talent. With mesmerizing singing, captivating dance performances, engaging drama, and a lively DJ night, the fest creates an atmosphere of pure joy and enthusiasm. It serves as a unifying platform, bringing students and guests together to revel in the diverse and vibrant cultural tapestry of UVCE. The positive energy and shared moments during Fiesta make it an unforgettable experience for everyone involved.

Date:5th Jan 2024                                   Venue:UVCE

Milagro

Milagro, UVCE's annual inter-collegiate cultural fest held during March-April, attracts up to 2,000 enthusiasts. This dynamic celebration transforms the campus into a vibrant hub of creativity, featuring diverse talents and creating an electric atmosphere. Milagro is more than a fest; it's a memorable celebration fostering unity and shared experiences among participants and attendees.

Date:17th Sep 2024                                   Venue:UVCE

KAGADA

Kagada, organized by IEEE UVCE, is a premier National Level Technical Paper Presentation Contest. Held annually, it attracts participants from across the nation, providing a platform for showcasing innovative research and ideas. This event reflects UVCE's commitment to fostering technical excellence and knowledge dissemination on a national scale

Date:10th Nov 2024                                   Venue:UVCE

INSPIRON

Inspiron, organized by UVCE's Training and Placement Office since 2009, is a pivotal annual event fostering connections between students and industry professionals. This platform provides valuable insights and opportunities for students, contributing to their career development and strengthening the bond between academia and industry.

Date:2nd Dec 2024                                   Venue:UVCE

NCODE

NCode, a monthly coding competition organized by IEEE UVCE Computer Society, is tailored exclusively for UVCE students to enhance their coding skills. This focused initiative provides a regular platform for participants to sharpen their coding prowess through engaging challenges. NCode stands as a valuable resource, contributing to the continuous improvement of students' coding abilities within the UVCE community.

Date:Everymonth                                   Venue:Online

CODEFURRY

CodeFury, IEEE UVCE Computer Society's Annual National Level Hackathon, is dedicated to solving major societal and industrial problems. Our vision is to make a significant impact through yearly hackathons, providing a platform for creative solutions and fostering collaboration among participants.

Date:22nd november 2024                   Venue:UVCE
